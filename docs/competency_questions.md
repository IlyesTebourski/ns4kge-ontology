# Competency Questions

## Use Case 1 — Structured exploration of the literature

*Goal: Identify recurrent experimental practices.*

- **CQ1:** Which datasets are most frequently used to evaluate negative sampling methods?
- **CQ2:** Which KGE models are most commonly associated with particular NS strategies?
- **CQ3:** Which evaluation metrics are typically reported?
- **CQ4:** Which combinations of dataset, KGE model, and NS method appear most frequently?

---

## Use Case 2 — Reporting coverage and comparability

*Goal: Assess completeness and comparability of experimental descriptions.*

- **CQ5:** Which experimental parameters are consistently reported across papers, and which are most frequently missing?
- **CQ6:** Which NS methods have not been evaluated across diverse datasets or KGE models?
- **CQ7:** Which articles do not fully describe their experimental configuration (optimizer, learning rate, loss function, hardware, NS ratio)?

---

## Use Case 3 — Exploration of experimental configurations

*Goal: Retrieve configurations to inform future experiment design.*

- **CQ8:** Which NS methods have been evaluated on a given dataset?
- **CQ9:** Which configurations are most frequently used for link-prediction tasks?
- **CQ10:** Which similar experimental configurations yield strongly diverging results across articles?
- **CQ11:** Which reported performance differences can be associated with changes in training configuration?
